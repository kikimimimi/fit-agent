# FitAgent Business Plan

## 1. Startup Name

FitAgent

Tagline: An AI personal fitness coach for personalized workout planning, progress tracking, and habit formation.

## 2. Problem

Fitness beginners often do not know how to create a safe and sustainable plan. Generic apps require too much manual setup, while general chatbots do not remember progress or provide structured workflows.

## 3. Target Users

- Fitness beginners.
- Students and busy young professionals.
- Home-training users.
- Light gym users.
- Users who want posture, body-shape, fat-loss support, or habit formation.

## 4. Solution

FitAgent generates structured weekly plans from goals, profile, equipment, schedule, and feedback. It remembers key preferences, tracks completion, and provides weekly review suggestions.

## 5. Product Features

Implemented:

- Profile intake.
- Rule-based weekly plan generation.
- Workout logs.
- Safety disclaimer and basic safety review.
- Agent workflow endpoint.
- SQLite memory and LLM call logs.

Future work:

- Real LLM coaching.
- Automatic plan adjustment.
- Mobile app.
- Progress analytics.
- Social or accountability features.

## 6. Market Opportunity

Assumption: fitness apps, AI coaching tools, and wellness subscriptions show demand for low-cost personalized coaching. This project does not claim verified market size, active users, revenue, or traction.

## 7. Business Model

- Freemium plan for basic workout generation.
- Paid subscription for adaptive plans, weekly reviews, and deeper memory.
- Student discount.
- Future B2B school wellness or campus fitness partnerships.

## 8. Pricing

Assumption-based MVP pricing:

- Free: limited plans and logs.
- Plus: low monthly subscription for adaptive plans and weekly reviews.
- Pro: advanced analytics and integrations.

No real revenue is claimed.

## 9. Go-to-market Strategy

- Start with students and beginner fitness communities.
- Share simple before-after habit dashboards, not medical claims.
- Publish beginner workout templates.
- Build campus ambassador or small creator partnerships.
- Use product-led growth through shareable weekly plans.

## 10. Competitive Advantage

- Conversational and beginner-friendly.
- Long-term memory and feedback loop.
- Lower complexity than manual tracking apps.
- Safer than a free-form chatbot because plan structure is rule-constrained.
- Indie product can move faster and focus on a narrow beginner segment.

## 11. Risks

- Health advice safety risk.
- LLM hallucination risk.
- User retention risk.
- High LLM cost if not cached.
- Competition from large fitness apps and general AI assistants.
- Regulatory and privacy requirements if the product expands.

## 12. Roadmap

MVP:

- Agent workflow, memory, logs, and docs.

Next:

- Feedback-driven plan adjustment.
- Weekly review UI.
- Real LLM provider with safety guardrails.
- Progress charts.

Future:

- Vector memory.
- Mobile app.
- Integrations with wearables.
- Production monitoring and billing.

## 13. LLM System Used

LLM system used: ChatGPT / Codex for development assistance and documentation/code generation in this workspace. Claude Code is referenced by local IDE settings, but this repository does not claim external experiment results or real production LLM deployment.

Safety note: FitAgent provides general fitness and habit guidance only. It does not diagnose, treat, or guarantee body-shape or posture correction.
